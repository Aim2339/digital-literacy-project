# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Social Media Do's

1. Maintain a professional profile  
2. Use strong passwords  
3. Verify information before sharing  
4. Respect others online  
5. Keep your profile updated  

---

## ❌ Social Media Don'ts

1. Do not share personal information  
2. Avoid posting offensive content  
3. Do not click suspicious links  
4. Avoid using weak passwords  
5. Do not engage in cyberbullying
