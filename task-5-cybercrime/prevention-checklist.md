# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist (For College Students)

## 🔐 Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. **Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords**  
   No bank, UPI app, or government official will ever ask for your OTP or PIN.

2. **Avoid scanning unknown QR codes**  
   Scanning a QR code can **send money**, not just receive it. Always verify before scanning.

3. **Check UPI collect requests carefully**  
   Decline requests from unknown numbers asking for payments on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.

4. **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)**  
   Turn on 2FA for Gmail, Instagram, banking apps, and other important accounts.

5. **Do not click suspicious links**  
   Fake scholarship, internship, and part-time job links often steal login credentials.

6. **Verify social media messages asking for money**  
   Friends' accounts may be hacked. Call them directly before sending money.

7. **Download apps only from official stores**  
   Use Google Play Store or Apple App Store. Avoid APK files shared via WhatsApp or Telegram.

8. **Use strong, unique passwords**  
   Use different passwords for banking, email, and social media accounts.

9. **Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments**  
   Public Wi-Fi networks can be unsafe and allow hackers to intercept data.

10. **Log out from shared devices**  
   Always log out after using college labs or cyber cafés.

---

## 💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

- Never approve **Collect Request** unless you personally initiated the transaction  
- Remember: **Receiving money does NOT require entering UPI PIN**

---

## 🚨 Report Cyber Crime Immediately

If you become a victim:

- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Cyber Crime Helpline:** **1930**  

Quick reporting increases chances of recovering lost money.
